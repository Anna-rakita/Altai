The Altai Mountains

Tourism to Altai is extraordinarily diverse. This unique land attracts tourists from all over the world! Altai, a vast mountainous territory, lies to the south of Siberia. Altai also has endless steppes, impenetrable taiga, numerous lakes, rivers and waterfalls. This diversity favours fauna species variety: brown bear, elk, wolf, golden eagle and others.

This region is great for active tourism. Plenty of hiking, bicycling and horse riding trails. Rafting down the rivers such as Katun, Chuya and Chulyshman will give you unforgettable memories. AltaiNature is happy to offer tours to Altai, price includes excursions, accommodation, food, meetings with local residents and other adventures.

Tour program

Chemal

Group gathering at the airport of Gorno-Altaisk city We will drive you to the hotel, located on the Katun river bank near Ust-Sema village. After check-in and a short rest, you'll have the first meeting with Altai.

Day 1.

Flora and fauna of Altai

You'll have an excursion to maral (The red deer) and horse farm. Between these points you'll visit Tavdinsky caves, there you'll have a walking through a cave to the viewpoint on the top of the mountain.

Day 2.

Katu Yaruk pass

Katu Yaruk is the most picturesque pass (available for reaching by car) in Altai. From the top, you'll see the canyon of the Chulyshman river and the Karasuu waterfall. On our way back, we will make a stop near the Pazyryk burials to explore the Pazyruk culture.

Day 3.

Chulyshman valley

One of the most beautiful places in this tour. We can't describe the beauty of this place. It's beyond words. The Stone Mushrooms - an amazing natural phenomenon. In a narrow canyon, there is a group of several dozen 5-7 meters tall "mushrooms". Tonight you'll stay in a tiny wooden tiny house, located in Chulyshman valley.
